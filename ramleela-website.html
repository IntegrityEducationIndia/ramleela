Our Story

Born From Life Itself

Ramleela Samajik Vikas Kendra is a community-driven NGO formed by people who deeply understand the challenges of rural Arghakhanchi, Nepal.

Shri Ram Bahadur Kunwar and his wife Mrs. Leela Devi began their life from Golkhada, Arghakhanchi — with limited resources but unlimited determination. Their dream was simple: a dignified livelihood and better future for their children through education and values.

परहित सरिस धर्म नहिं भाई

There is no greater dharma than serving others.

— The Ramayana

From this lived experience emerged our Kendra — an initiative proving that if one family can rise through hard work and education, every family can.

Today, our students work and thrive in Nepal, India, Australia, New Zealand, and the United States — guided at every stage by our dedicated skill mentors.

Programs That Transform Lives

Practical skills that equip young people to succeed locally and globally.

Basic Computer Skills Program

Running in two schools in Arghakhanchi — Shri Argharajsthal Madhyamik Vidhyalya (Argha Raisechoor & Pakale Kimdada). Each school serves 45+ active students.

Medical Lab Facility

Setting up accessible lab testing facilities near local health posts — ending the need for vulnerable families to travel all the way to Sandhikharka for basic tests.

Founder's Message

A Message From Our Heart

Namaskar. I come from the same soil, the same villages, and the same struggles as many of you reading this. Ramleela Samajik Vikas Kendra was not created overnight — it is the result of decades of learning, sacrifice, and reflection.

My parents, Shri Ram Bahadur Kunwar and Mrs. Leela Devi, taught us that honest hard work never goes unrewarded. What they achieved for their children, we now wish to extend to every child and family in our region.

धैर्यं सर्वत्र साधनम्

Patience and perseverance are the keys to success.

— Sanskrit Proverb

I invite you to join us — not just as supporters, but as partners in this journey. Together, let us build a future where dignity, service, and compassion guide our actions.

This is our shared Ramleela. 🙏
